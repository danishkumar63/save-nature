Why?

Trees Provide Oxygen.

In one year an acre of mature trees can provide enough oxygen for 18 people.

treepeople.org

Trees Combat Climate Change.

In one year, an acre of mature trees absorbs the same amount of CO2 produced when you drive your car 26,000 miles.

treepeople.org

Trees Reduce Stress.

Trees provide beauty and help people feel serene, peaceful, restful, and tranquil. Green spaces can also help lower the amount of criminal activity in a community.

treesaregood.org

How?

Plant a tree.

“ The best time to plant a tree is twenty years ago. The second best time is now. Chinese Proverb

Cause

Deforestation

Deforestation is one of the major causes of climate change. The loss of trees and other vegetation can cause climate change, desertification, soil erosion, fewer crops, flooding, increased greenhouse gases in the atmosphere, and a host of problems for indigenous people.
